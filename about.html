[ OptiBuild Origin ]

Why we built OptiBuild

OptiBuild started as an internal response to repeated uncertainty in early-stage project conversations. Developers had drawings, rough assumptions, and pressure to move quickly, but no consistent way to stress-test financial and execution risk before committing. We built OptiBuild so teams can evaluate feasibility with sharper numbers and stronger confidence.
